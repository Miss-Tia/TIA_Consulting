The Process

What working together looks like.

Every IT Consulting engagement follows a clear structure so you always know what's happening and why.

  1. 01

    Discovery Call

    A free 30-minute conversation about your goals, current setup, and what's feeling off. No pressure, no pitch.

  2. 02

    Technology Audit

    I review your tools, subscriptions, infrastructure, and how they're being used day-to-day, to surface gaps, redundancies, and risks.

  3. 03

    Analysis & Recommendations

    I map what's working, what isn't, and what changes would make the biggest difference, prioritized by impact and feasibility.

  4. 04

    Roadmap Delivery

    You receive a written, prioritized plan with clear next steps, timelines, and budget guidance you can actually act on.

  5. 05

    Review & Handoff

    We walk through the deliverable together, answer questions, and you leave with a plan that's yours to own and execute.

Is this right for you?

This work is for the right companies and organizations.

A good fit if…

  • You're evaluating new tools and want an outside perspective
  • Your systems feel scattered, outdated, or hard to explain
  • You're planning a migration, rebuild, or significant technology shift
  • You want honest recommendations that are tailored to your needs rather than a sales pitch
  • You need a written plan to follow and refer back to
  • Your organization values clarity

May not be the right fit if…

  • You need someone to make the decision for you without discussion
  • You're looking for the cheapest vendor, not the right one
  • Leadership isn't open to changing how technology is managed
  • You want a quick fix rather than a strategic plan
