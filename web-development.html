The Approach

Web development at TIA Consulting is about building sites that serve real people like your audience, your staff, and your organization as a whole. The Process

What a build looks like from start to finish.

Every web project follows the same clear phases with no surprises, and no scope creep.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We talk through your goals, your audience, your existing site (if any), and what success looks like. I ask a lot of questions here so the build starts with the right foundation.

  2. 02

    Content & Structure

    Before any design happens, we nail down what the site needs to say and how it's organized. Good structure makes everything else easier and prevents expensive revisions later.

  3. 03

    Design & Build

    I design and build in tandem, sharing progress along the way. You're not waiting until the end to see what you're getting.

  4. 04

    Review & Revisions

    You review the full build, give feedback, and we make refinements. Two rounds of revisions are included in every build engagement.

  5. 05

    Launch & Handoff

    I handle the launch, document everything you need to maintain the site, and do a full walkthrough so you're not left guessing how anything works.
