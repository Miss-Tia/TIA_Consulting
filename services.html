How I Work

TIA Consulting supports mission-driven work and uses technology as a force for good. I do not accept projects that promote harassment, hate, discrimination, or dehumanization of any group, or that are intended to intimidate, exclude, or target people for who they are.

If your work is rooted in dignity, safety, and positive impact, you’ll feel right at home here. If this boundary feels misaligned with your goals, we may not be the right fit...and that’s okay.
