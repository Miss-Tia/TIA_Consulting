The Process

What working on NationBuilder together looks like.

Every NationBuilder engagement is built around the same principle: no surprises, and no setups your team can't maintain.

  1. 01

    Discovery Call

    We talk through your organization, your goals on NationBuilder, and what's working or not working in your current setup.

  2. 02

    Platform Audit

    I do a thorough review of your NationBuilder instance — or, for new organizations, a review of your existing data and workflows before we build anything.

  3. 03

    Strategy & Planning

    Based on the audit, we agree on a clear plan: what gets built, in what order, and why.

  4. 04

    Build & Configure

    I handle the hands-on work of configuration, data cleanup, theme development, and workflow setup, and then share progress at key milestones.

  5. 05

    Training & Handoff

    Your team gets a live training session, full documentation, and a 30-day window for follow-up questions.

Is this right for you?

NationBuilder support for the right organizations.

A good fit if…

  • You're a nonprofit, campaign, or advocacy organization using (or planning to use) NationBuilder
  • Your NationBuilder instance feels messy, confusing, or underused
  • Your team is afraid to touch certain parts of the platform
  • Data quality is a problem due to duplicates, bad tags, unreliable lists
  • You want your team to actually understand the platform after we're done

May not be the right fit if…

  • You're not willing to invest in cleaning up data before building on top of it
  • The root cause of your data problems is how data gets collected, and that's not something you're open to addressing
  • You can't provide access to DNS and domain settings...email deliverability work requires it
  • You want someone to hand you a finished product without involving your team in the process
  • You're looking for the lowest possible price regardless of quality or outcomes
