The Approach

Clarity over complexity.

Process and systems consulting is about stepping back from the day-to-day and asking: why is this so hard? Usually, the answer isn't that your team isn't capable, it's that the workflows, tools, and responsibilities around them haven't been designed with intention.

I work with organizations and companies to map out what's happening, identify where things break down, and build something that actually fits how your team works. All of this without unnecessary complexity or tools for the sake of tools.

The Process

What working together looks like.

Every engagement follows the same clear structure so you always know what's happening, what's next, and what to expect.

  1. 01

    Discovery Call

    We start with a free 30-60 minute conversation. You share what's going on, I ask questions, and together we figure out if this is the right fit and where to begin.

  2. 02

    Assessment

    I review your existing workflows, tools, team structure, and documentation — asking the questions that help surface what's really happening versus what's supposed to be happening.

  3. 03

    Strategy & Design

    Based on the findings, we develop a clear plan. For redesigns and implementations, the deliverables are documented workflows, tool recommendations, and a phased roadmap your team can actually follow.

  4. 04

    Build & Implement

    For Implementation engagements, I handle the hands-on setup with configuring tools, migrating data, and testing workflows before anything goes live.

  5. 05

    Handoff & Support

    You receive full documentation, a training session for your team, and a 30-day check-in to make sure everything is running as expected. You leave with a system you understand and can maintain.

Is this right for you?

This work is for the right organizations and companies.

A good fit if…

  • Your team spends more time managing tools than doing the actual work
  • You've grown faster than your processes have
  • Responsibilities are unclear or things regularly fall through the cracks
  • You've tried to fix it internally and keep running into the same problems
  • You want a documented system, not just advice
  • Your work is mission-driven and you value clarity and dignity

May not be the right fit if…

  • You're looking for a quick fix with no organizational change
  • Leadership isn't on board with redesigning how things work
  • You need enterprise-scale infrastructure (this is boutique, intentional work)
